We can help with anxiety and depression.

Using Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), we teach practical coping strategies that replace negative self-talk and avoidant behaviors. Your child learns to manage their emotions and re-engage with the things that matter — school, friendships, family life. We also provide parent training so you have the tools to support progress at home.

Specialized ABA therapy for autism.

Our Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) conduct comprehensive developmental evaluations using evidence-based assessments like VB-MAPP and ABLLS-R. We create individualized treatment plans targeting skill deficits across all developmental domains — delivered in-home and at school. Social skills groups are also available for peer interaction practice.

Schedule an Evaluation

Evidence-based behavior support.

Behavior disorders like ODD, ADHD, and conduct challenges respond well to structured intervention. We use a combination of ABA and behavior therapy techniques to identify what's driving the behavior, teach replacement strategies, and build skills for emotional regulation. Treatment is delivered across home, school, and community settings.

Support through change.

Whether it's a new school, college transition, divorce, or building independence as a young adult — change is hard. Through counseling, we help individuals develop coping skills, find clarity, and move forward with confidence. Online sessions are available from the comfort of home.

Our Approach

How we create lasting change.

Step 01
Understand

We start with a comprehensive evaluation — learning your child's history, your concerns, and your goals. No assumptions, no one-size-fits-all.

Step 02
Build the Plan

Questions

What parents ask us.

We provide services for children, teens, and young adults. Our ABA services focus on children with autism and developmental disabilities, while our counseling services extend through young adulthood.
ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis) focuses on teaching new skills and reducing challenging behaviors through structured, data-driven techniques — primarily for individuals with autism. Counseling addresses emotional and mental health concerns like anxiety, depression, and life transitions using approaches like CBT. Lauren is qualified to provide both.
ABA therapy is delivered in-home and at school — wherever your child is. Counseling sessions are available in-person or online through our secure telehealth platform. We meet you where it's most convenient and effective.
Please contact us directly to discuss insurance coverage and payment options. We're happy to walk you through the process and help you understand your benefits.
Send us an email at Scheduling@risingmindstherapy.com with your name, phone number, the age of the client, and a brief description of your concerns. We'll reach out to discuss next steps and schedule an initial consultation.
For ABA, we begin with a parent meeting to learn about your child, followed by a comprehensive developmental evaluation. For counseling, the first session focuses on understanding your history, concerns, and goals — then building a personalized treatment plan together.
